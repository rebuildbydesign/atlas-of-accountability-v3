HOW TO USE THIS INTERACTIVE MAP

1) CLICK ON A COUNTY to view the number of major disaster declarations, post-disaster federal funding, and detailed information about your congressional and state representatives.

2) SEARCH AN ADDRESS located at the top right corner of the map to enter a specific address. This will reveal county-level data and provide information about the corresponding congressional and state representatives.

3) NAVIGATE THE MAP by clicking and dragging with your mouse. Zoom in and out to explore other parts of the country.

4) TAKE ACTION by contacting your representatives. If you are a representative, collaborate with other policymakers whose constituents are experiencing increased and severe disasters. Together, we can advocate for stronger, more resilient infrastructure in our communities
